Built in Godot 4.5


**Controlls:**

**Spaceship:**
  W - Pitch Up.
  S - Pitch Down.
  A - Yaw Left.
  D - Yaw Right.
  Q - Roll Left.
  E - Roll Right.
  Caps Lock - Increase Thrusters.
  Shift - Decrease Thrusters.

**Player:**
  W - Move Forward.
  S - Move Backward.
  A - Turn Left.
  D - Turn Right.
  Q - Strafe Left.
  E - Strafe Right.

  F - Enter / Exit Ship.
